# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Pokedex Markets welcomes constructive, good-faith participation in this testnet-only engineering repository. Contributors, maintainers, and community participants are expected to communicate professionally, focus critique on ideas and code, respect differing perspectives, and prioritize the safety of users and the integrity of the project.

## Expected behavior

Participants should use clear technical reasoning, disclose relevant conflicts where appropriate, accept feedback in good faith, and avoid claims that overstate the safety, valuation, or readiness of the contracts. Discussions should remain relevant to the repository's limited scope: testnet contribution receipts, evidence commitments, and market catalogue operations.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, deliberate disruption, publication of private credentials, and knowingly misleading security or financial claims are not acceptable. Suspected security issues must not be posted publicly; follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close contributions, or restrict participation when behavior conflicts with these standards. Reports about conduct concerns may be made through the repository's private security-reporting channel or the maintainer contact path published with the canonical repository. Reports will be handled as discreetly as practical.
